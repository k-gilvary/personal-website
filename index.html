Hey, I'm Kenny! I am a developer out of New Jersey, US. My passion for web development started a long time ago, when I was about 10 or 11. My friend at the time told me about this website called Codecademy, a place that taught you how to build a web page entirely for free (these were simpler times). I only learned the most basic HTML and CSS, but that experience opened many other doors for me. I found this passion extended far past building websites, and into building in general. Making my ideas a reality became THE most fulfilling aspect of my life, and it still is today.

Currently, my best languages are HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. However, I am constantly learning something new. Gradually, I am trying to learn Java, Go, AWS, C#, and more. On top of these languages, I am also trying to learn things to add to my hobby collection: mastering guitar, music production, 3D modelling and animating, ui/ux design, game design and development, and cybersecurity.

If you are someone in need of a new, dedicated, and driven web developer, please do not hesitate to contact me!
